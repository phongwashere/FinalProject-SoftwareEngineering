About Tunes

Tunes is the site for the music lovers who have a desire to discover music differently. There are plenty of sites that will give the basic information for an artist but Tunes is much more than that. Tunes keeps it casual and fun for those who want to listen to covers of their favorite artist, for those who don't know what to listen to but have an idea, and for those who want to listen to a completely new genre.
From the landing page, you can find the top songs for each genre. Each song will redirect you to a page where you can listen to covers for that song. After signing in, if you want recommended artists based on a song you already have in mind, take a look at the reccomendations page. The randomize page will help you find a completely random song, regardless of genre or artist. And you can add all these newly discovered songs and artists in the favorites page.
